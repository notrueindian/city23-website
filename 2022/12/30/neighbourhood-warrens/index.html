Neighborhood: The Warrens

New construction, natural erosion, magical interference, for whatever reason sometimes the river of time shifts its course, leaving dry or damp canal beds empty. This network of ditches and tunnels has become home to the poor and downtrodden of the city. Digging their homes and businesses into the sides of the canals the often unearthed treasures of the past and future, though most of what was there to find has long been ecscavated. These peculiar anachronism, when too complicated or large to be sold to or stolen by the city’s elites have become part of the architechture of the Warrens.

True name: Deep Burroughs, shadowed anachronism.
